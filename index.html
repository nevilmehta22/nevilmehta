My Story

Hey there! Thanks for visiting me. Its really nice to have someone hear you out :)

I am Nevil Mehta, an aspiring Data Analyst!

To begin with, I started my academic journey as a Mechanical Engineer, followed by a year of work exp as a Shop Floor trainee and later completed my Master's in Mechatronics & Robotics.

Now, you might be wondering, "How did I move from engineering to data analysis?" Well, the answer is quite simple.

During my phase as an Engineer, I spent countless hours working with complex systems, all driven by data from sensors and machines. Analyzing this data to predict failures, optimize processes, and make informed decisions became a crucial part of my work. This hands-on experience with technical problem-solving and programming naturally led me to see the power of data analytics.

Please feel free to browse through my journey as a Data Analyst. Being an amateur in the field, I would really love to hear from an expert like you to improve myself even further. In case you have any suggestions or recommendations for me, you can contact me anytime! Thanks a lot for spending your lovely time :)
